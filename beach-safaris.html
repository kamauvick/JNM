Diani

A stunning unspoiled crystal white sandy beach, fringed by coral reefs and a tidal salt water lake. All combined with the loving care of the Kenyan people will make your beach-vacation relaxing and memorable.

Things to do in Diani:

  • Sky Diving
  • Visit Shimba hills National Reserve
  • Day visit to Wasini Island

Watamu "Home of sweet people"

Watamu is a village on the Kenyan coast 20km south of Malindi, its shorelines features idyllic white sandy beaches and turquoise waters. The area is protected as part of the watamu marine national park and has one of the best snokelling and diving locations on the coast of Africa.

Places to visit:

  • Arabuko Sokoke
  • Gede Ruins
  • Vasco da Gama Pillar
  • Watamu/Malindi marine park

Rusinga Island

Rusinga island sits in the fresh waters of lake Victoria at the mouth of Winam gulf. It is a place of infinite beauty to calm down and rejuvenate the body. It is an important destination to learn about the history of early man because one of the aerliest fossils was discoverred there.

Things to do:

  • Guided tour around Rusinga and Mfangano islands
  • Cultural performances
  • Boat races
  • Bird watching (Over 300 species)

Masai Mara

The MAASAI MARA is one of the world's most prolific game areas and home to the BIG 5 with mind blowing sightings all year round. The Mara is famous for it's exceptional population of Big cats and the annual migration of wildebeest from the Serengeti every year from July to October. Samburu National Reserve

The SAMBURU NATIONAL RESERVE is an area of spectacular scenery ; it's dry plains bisected by the meandering Ewaso Nyiro river with the back drop of My Kenya. It was one of the areas in which conservationists George and Joy Adamson raised Elsa the lioness made famous in the best selling book and award winning movie BORN FREE.

Crowned by mount Kilimanjaro, Africas highest mountain(5895m) and the highest free standing mountain in the world, Amboseli National park is famous for it's large herds of elephants that offers best upclose experience making it one of the popular safari destinations in Africa.
